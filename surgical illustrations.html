Ligament Reconstruction and Tendon Interposition (LRTI) for Thumb CMC Arthritis

The purpose of these series of illustrations is to effectively visually communicate a surgical procedure that we observed and later researched.

Continue reading to learn about the process that went into creating this piece.

Sketches

After observing the surgical procedure performed by a resident at SickKid's Hospital, we drafted sketches of the different stages. Further refinements were conduced to ensure there was effective storytelling throughout the piece.

Final Illustrations

The following illustrations are the final renderings of the surgical procedure: ligament reconstruction and tendon interposition (LRTI) for Thumb CMC Arthritis.
